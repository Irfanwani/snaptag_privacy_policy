Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 6, 2024

Welcome to Snap Tag! Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when using our geo tagging app.

1. Information We Collect

Snap Tag does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not include advertisements, and no third-party tracking services are used.

2. Permissions

To provide the core functionality of Snap Tag, we request the following permissions:

  • Location Access: Used to geo-tag the photos with your current location.
  • Camera Access: Required to take photos for geo-tagging.
  • Media Library Access: Allows saving and accessing photos in your device's media gallery.

These permissions are used solely for their stated purpose within the app and are never shared or transmitted externally.

3. Data Usage

Snap Tag does not collect any form of personal or non-personal data. All photos and location tags remain on your device and are not uploaded to our servers or shared with any third party.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any updates.
